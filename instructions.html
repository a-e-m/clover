Instructions

Word Entering Mode

Look at the two bottom-most pair of words, i.e. the two words by the green bracket. Use the text box at the bottom of the screen to enter one word that somehow connects them or describes them both. Proper nouns, acronyms, onomatopoeia, and compound words are allowed, as long as they're a single word. Try to stick to English (no just translating a word into another language). Once you've thought of a word, press "Enter" to submit it.

You can tap the button in the middle of the cards to rotate the cards. This will let you cycle through all four word pairs and enter four words. Once you've entered four words, a popup will let you copy a link to send this arrangment to others.

Card Placing Mode

If you receive a completed link from someone, you can try to figure out how their cards were originally arranged. Try to match up each word around the four edges with a pair of words on the matching edges of two cards. To make things harder, an additional fifth card has been added in, and the positions and rotations of cards have been randomized.

You can tap the button in the middle of the cards to rotate all of the cards. You can tap a specific card to rotate it individually. If you have a mouse, you can drag and drop cards to swap their positions. If you're using a touchscreen, you can long-press a card (it will change colors to indicate it's selected), and then you can tap another card to swap their positions.
